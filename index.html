F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who are Experts and Mentors?

    These are people who know about a given subject in detail. Say if you consult in a topic like Study Abroad, your mentor can be a Student who has been accepted into Ivy leagues and been through the process or actual consultants. Talkin provides the profile and credentials of a given person so that you can choose whom you want to get Talkin' with.

  • How does Talkin ensure mentors are genuine?

    The mentors are tested on certain parameters, before we list them on your platform. A supervisor from our teams overlooks the consultation to ensure quality time and talk.

  • How is Talkin different?

    We bring the best of the best to advice you in the best way possible, in the widest range of areas. Talkin is the one stop solution for your problems, it isn't Google or a Blog where one-advice-fits-all. This is a completely personalised experience. If we miss the category you've looking for advice in, you may request us; we'll short assign you a mentor.

  • How can I become a mentor ?

    Find the link below to fill a form that lets us know, you're willing to be a mentor. We'll get back to you, shortly.
